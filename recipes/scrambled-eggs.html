Scrambled Eggs

Recipe created by Kitchen Kitty

Back to Homepage

Description:

These scrambled eggs with cottage cheese are creamy, fluffy, and make a delicious change from regular scrambled eggs. Perfect with a slice of tomato and turkey bacon for a delicious and fast low-carb breakfast.

Ingredients:

  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 4 large eggs, beaten
  • Â¼ cup cottage cheese
  • 1 teaspoon chopped fresh chives, or to taste (Optional)
  • ground black pepper to taste

Steps:

  1. Melt butter in a skillet over medium heat. Pour beaten eggs into the skillet; let cook undisturbed until the bottom of the eggs begins to firm, 1 to 2 minutes.
  2. Stir cottage cheese and chives into eggs and season with black pepper.
  3. Cook and stir until eggs are nearly set, 3 to 4 minutes more.
